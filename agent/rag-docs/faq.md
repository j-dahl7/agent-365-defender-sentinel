# Contoso Cloud - Customer FAQ

## How do I reset my password?

Visit https://contoso.com/reset and follow the prompts. You'll receive a reset
link at the email address associated with your account.

## How do I check my order status?

Ask the assistant: "What's the status of order ORD-998?"

## How do I upgrade my plan?

Ask the assistant to upgrade your plan, or visit https://contoso.com/billing.

## Who can I contact for enterprise support?

Email enterprise-support@contoso.com or call 1-800-CONTOSO.
